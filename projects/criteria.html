Overview

The dynamic criteria engine is a context‑aware layer that allows marketers to build flexible segments and triggers by combining user traits, event attributes and custom entities. This engine powers personalized campaigns and merchandising rules at scale.

My Role

I designed the criteria builder from the ground up — shaping the data grammar, collaborating with engineering on performance considerations, and leading user research to ensure the UI was both powerful and easy to use. I then layered recommendation algorithms on top of the criteria results to deliver relevant content and offers.

Outcome

Beta campaigns built with the new criteria engine delivered double‑digit lifts in engagement. Clients now create and iterate on complex targeting rules in minutes, unlocking more effective personalization strategies.
