Overview

The hybrid builder combines visual blocks with a code editor to give users the best of both worlds: intuitive drag‑and‑drop for structure and inline scripting for advanced logic. This approach dramatically speeds up solution creation while maintaining flexibility.

My Role

I oversaw the product vision and execution for the hybrid builder. Through A/B tests and user research, we discovered that a combined visual/code experience reduced build time significantly versus typing‑only workflows. I collaborated with designers on the new UI language and guided engineers on modular architecture and component reuse.

Outcome

Users reported significantly faster solution delivery and fewer mistakes. The modernised visual hierarchy also improved onboarding and adoption rates while refreshing the look and feel of the platform.
