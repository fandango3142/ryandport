Overview

At Xfinite, we pioneered AI‑generated alternative endings for films, opening new possibilities for storytelling and monetisation. By using generative models, we created multiple narrative paths that users could experience in the theatre or on OTT platforms.

My Role

I defined the product strategy for generative AI in entertainment and worked closely with creative teams to integrate AI outputs seamlessly into the filmmaking process. I also led the negotiation with content partners and steered the launch of the first theatrical release with an AI‑generated ending.

Outcome

The first AI‑enabled release generated significant buzz and served as proof of concept for further productions. By demonstrating the potential of AI‑driven content, we unlocked new revenue streams and inspired similar initiatives across the industry.
