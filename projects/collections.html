Collections Engine

Dynamic catalog builder for personalization

Back to Projects

Overview

The collections engine aggregates product catalogs into flexible, rule‑driven groups that can be surfaced across channels. By defining criteria such as replenishment frequency, cross‑sell affinity and trending attributes, marketers can assemble collections that adapt to user context and inventory.

My Role

I formulated the concept, created user flows, and partnered with designers and engineers to craft a self‑serve UI for building collections. My work included prioritizing use cases, modelling relationship logic and ensuring the system could scale across millions of items.

Outcome

Collections have become foundational to personalization strategies, powering replenishment, cross‑sell and merchandising campaigns. Clients report higher click‑through rates on recommendation widgets and streamlined operations for managing large catalogs.
