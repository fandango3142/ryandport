Overview

Vibe‑coding is my approach to quickly turning ideas into tangible prototypes. Instead of relying solely on wireframes, I write working front‑end code — often in React or vanilla HTML/CSS — to convey the intended experience. This allows stakeholders to interact with a concept early and provide more meaningful feedback.

My Role

Whenever I need to de‑risk a design or alignment on a complex interaction, I jump into code. For example, while exploring the hybrid builder, I built multiple prototypes to test different workflows and UI paradigms. These prototypes were used in user studies and helped decide the final direction.

Outcome

By leveraging vibe‑coding, my teams accelerate decision making and reduce miscommunication. Prototypes have led to cleaner designs, smoother development handoffs and better stakeholder buy‑in.
